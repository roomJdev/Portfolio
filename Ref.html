About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student in HXRLAB at Kyung Hee University, advised by Prof. UiJong Ju. My main interests are in software engineering and cognitive science, especially in using VR to study and improve user experiences. I'm particularly focused on researching how to make processing of VR devices more energy-efficient with foveated rendering and finding ways to improve safety for people while they drive or walk.
